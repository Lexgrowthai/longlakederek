Principal Dentist & Founder

Dr. Derek Lewis

Dr. Derek Lewis graduated from the University of Toronto with a Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree in 1997. His undergraduate studies were obtained in Kinesiology at the University of Waterloo. Dr. Lewis has been practicing dentistry in Sudbury since 2010, and worked on Manitoulin Island from 1997 to 2014.

Dr. Lewis strives to continually improve his dental practice with the newest technology. His skills are upgraded continually with the help of ongoing courses to better serve his patients. He is a member of the RCDS (Royal College of Dental Surgeons), ODA (Ontario Dental Association), SDDS (Sudbury District Dental Society), and is a Fellow of the International Congress of Oral Implantology.

Dr. Lewis was born in Ottawa, Ontario. He grew up in Wikwemikong on Manitoulin Island and returned there to work when he graduated as a Doctor of Dental Surgery. He began working in Sudbury in 2010 at Long Lake Family Dentistry and maintains the Wikwemikong Dental Clinic with the help of a dental associate.

Over more than twenty years living and working in Northern Ontario, Dr. Lewis has maintained a deep appreciation for the natural beauty of the area he grew up in. The Long Lake Dental Clinic was recently moved to a larger and updated office space — staying up to date with current technology and techniques is part of a commitment to bring optimal care to every patient.

Biography

Dr. Derek Lewis — A Life in Dentistry

Dr. Lewis continues to take courses and learn about the profession he started many years ago, and looks forward to continuing to work with patients of all ages to care for their oral health needs. His dedication to lifelong learning ensures that Long Lake Family Dentistry remains at the forefront of modern dental care in Sudbury.

Early Life

Born in Ottawa, Raised in Wikwemikong

Dr. Lewis was born in Ottawa, Ontario, and grew up in Wikwemikong on Manitoulin Island — a community he has remained deeply connected to throughout his career.

University of Waterloo

BSc in Kinesiology

Dr. Lewis completed his undergraduate degree in Kinesiology at the University of Waterloo, building a strong foundation in human biology and health sciences.

1997

Doctor of Dental Surgery — University of Toronto

Dr. Lewis graduated with his DDS degree and returned to Wikwemikong on Manitoulin Island to begin serving the community where he grew up.

1997 – 2014

Manitoulin Island Practice

Practiced dentistry on Manitoulin Island for over 17 years, providing essential dental care to Northern Ontario communities. He continues to maintain the Wikwemikong Dental Clinic with the help of a dental associate.

2010

Long Lake Family Dentistry — Sudbury

Dr. Lewis and his wife Amanda moved to Sudbury with their two boys Jadyn and Ryan, establishing Long Lake Family Dentistry and becoming an integral part of the Sudbury community.

Present
