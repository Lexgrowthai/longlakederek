Professional Teeth Cleaning

The cornerstone of a healthy smile — thorough, gentle, and essential for patients of all ages.

Why Professional Teeth Cleaning Matters

A healthy smile begins long before you notice any discomfort. At Long Lake Family Dentistry, we believe that preventive care is the most powerful investment you can make in your oral health — and professional teeth cleaning is at the very heart of that philosophy. Recommended every six months for most patients, a professional cleaning goes far beyond what even the most diligent daily brushing and flossing can achieve at home.

Our experienced dental hygienists provide gentle, thorough cleanings in a relaxed, modern environment.

Over time, even with excellent brushing habits, plaque accumulates in the hard-to-reach spaces between teeth, along the gumline, and behind molars. When plaque is not removed promptly, it hardens into a substance called tartar — also known as calculus — which cannot be eliminated by a toothbrush alone. Left untreated, tartar becomes a breeding ground for the bacteria responsible for gum disease, cavities, tooth sensitivity, and eventual tooth loss. Our skilled dental hygienists use professional-grade instruments to safely and thoroughly remove tartar deposits, giving your mouth a clean foundation on which your daily home care routine can truly excel.

But a professional cleaning is about far more than just removing buildup. Each appointment is a comprehensive preventive health visit. While your hygienist cleans your teeth, they are also carefully evaluating the health of your gums, identifying early warning signs of decay, checking for changes in your soft tissue, and monitoring bone levels through up-to-date digital imaging. Catching problems early — before they escalate into costly, complex treatments — is always better for your health and your wallet.

The connection between oral health and overall systemic health is well-established in medical research. Chronic gum inflammation has been linked to an increased risk of heart disease, diabetes complications, respiratory illness, and adverse pregnancy outcomes. By maintaining healthy gums through regular professional cleanings, you are not just protecting your teeth — you are making a meaningful contribution to your whole-body wellness.

What to Expect at Your Appointment

1

Health History Review

We begin by reviewing any updates to your medical history, medications, or dental concerns since your last visit. This ensures your care is always tailored to your current health status.

2

Comprehensive Oral Examination

Your hygienist will gently probe your gum pockets, assess gum tissue health, check for signs of decay, and evaluate any existing restorations such as fillings or crowns.

3

Scaling & Tartar Removal

Using ultrasonic and hand instruments, we carefully remove all plaque and tartar deposits from the surfaces of your teeth and below the gumline — the areas daily brushing simply cannot reach.

4

Polishing & Stain Removal

We finish with a professional polish using a gentle abrasive paste that removes surface stains and leaves your teeth feeling smooth, clean, and noticeably brighter.

5

Fluoride Treatment & Home Care Guidance

A fluoride application helps strengthen enamel and protect against sensitivity. We also take the time to review brushing and flossing techniques personalized to your specific needs.
